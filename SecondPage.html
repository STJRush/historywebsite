Historians and Archeologists

Historians are a bit like detectives. They try to piece together the story of what happened from the evidence available.

In Junior Certificate History, "Historians and Archeologists" is the first topic to be covered

In this topic, we learn about first and secondary sources and how archeologists work on a site.

We also learn about how Historians date artifacts.
