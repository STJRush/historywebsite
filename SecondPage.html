1st year

Historians and acheologists

    Historians are a bit like detectives. They try to piece together the story of what happened from the evidence available.

    In Junior Certificate History, "Historians and Archeologists" is the first topic to be covered

    In this topic, we learn about first and secondary sources and how archeologists work on a site.

    We also learn about how Historians date artifacts.

    Ancient Ireland

    In Ancient Ireland, you will learn about Ireland in its early years

    You will also learn about how they lived, the homes they built, how they worked as a community and the tombs in which they were buried

    The case study for this chapter is Newgrange, an ancient burial tomb built in 3500BC, making it older than the Pyramids
