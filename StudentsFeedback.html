Students Feedback

While making this website, we decided to ask the students questions about learning history at St Josephs Rush.

Here's what they said:

Interview with Luke

Question 1. What do you like about History?

There is a lot of interesting things on the course

Questions 2. How do you fell on the new Junior Cert getting rid of certain topics?

The first year part isn't needed so much but should keep revolutions and plantations

Question 3. What was the most interesting topic you learned?

Irish history, because you learn about what happened in your country

Question 4. Will you be doing Leaving Cert history and why?

Yes, because I feel there are more parts I would like to know about

Interview with James

Question 1. What do you like about History?

Question 4. Will you be doing Leaving Cert history and why?
